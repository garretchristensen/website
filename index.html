I'm Garret Christensen. I received my economics PhD from UC Berkeley in 2011. I am presently an economist with the US Census Bureau. Before that I was a project scientist with the Berkeley Initiative for Transparency in the Social Sciences and a Data Science Fellow with the Berkeley Institute for Data Science.
